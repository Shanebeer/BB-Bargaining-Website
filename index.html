About Us

BB Sales & Storage

At BB Sales & Storage, we breathe new life into your pre-loved treasures. From cozy sofas to cutting-edge electronics, we showcase a diverse range of second-hand gems. Our mission is ensuring your once-cherished possessions find eager new owners while we keep them safe in our secure facility.

BB Removals

BB Removals sweeps away your outdoor clutter. We're your garden's best friend, whisking away stubborn tree stumps and mountains of leaves. Picture your yard transformed from jungle to oasis in hours. No more weekend trips to the dump - we'll haul off your garden waste and unwanted items, leaving you with a clean slate and peace of mind.

BB Transport

BB Transport turns daunting relocations into smooth journeys. From king-size beds to giant refrigerators, we're the alphabet of big-item transit - moving you from A to Z with ease. Watch as your mammoth couch glides effortlessly to its new home, while you sit back and relax. We handle the heavy lifting, so your life's big pieces fall perfectly into place.
